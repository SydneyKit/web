About the Comic Book

"It's okay to not be okay." That is something that is not said enough. Love, Me serves as a collection of short comics that share advice and personal stories from friends and family. Each comic tackles subject such as depression, sexuality, deaf awareness, and more, in hopes that others can find comfort in relatability.

Love, Me started off as a semester-long project for Webcomics and has continued into a larger series.
