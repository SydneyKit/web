About the Comic Books

"It's okay to not be okay." That is something that is not said enough. Love, Me serves as a collection of short comics that share advice and personal stories from friends and family. Each comic tackles subject such as depression, sexuality, facing your fears, and more, in hopes that others can find comfort in relatability.


How It Started

Love, Me started off as a semester-long project for Webcomics and has continued into a larger series. The larger series consists of two published comic books, merchandise, and a mini series.
