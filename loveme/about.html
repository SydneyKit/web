About the Comic Books

Love, Me is a continuing community project that is comprised of collections of short stories based on submitted advice. As of December 2021, the series features three self-publihsed comic books and twenty-three originally designed, featured animal characters.


How It Started

Love, Me started off as a semester-long project for an undergraduate class and has continued into a larger series. The course was taught by Mitch Vicieux who is now the co-founder, along with Sydney Kit, of the Columbus-based comic club CX3.

It continued outside of class projects and into a second volume, which saw the addition of Candy Princehorn as the script creator. Candy continues to work as the script creator for the series.
