Webcomics

Webcomics was a class taken SP21 that featured a weekly comic and other exercises. The class was taught by Mitch Vicieux, whom Sydney Kit credits the series to. Sydney Kit received a grant to help publish the weekly comic into a comic book.


This is an ongoing series which can be found at the Love, Me page.

Copies of the comic book can be purchased at the shop. If you have a digital access code for the digital version you can access it here.
