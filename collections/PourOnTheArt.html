What is Pour On The Art?

Sydney Kit is a paint and sip instructor in the Columbus branch of Pour On The Art. These are 3-hour classes for beginners! No experience is necessary, and all supplies are included with artist instruction. All you have to do is show up, sit down at your canvas, and prepare yourself for an amazing Paint Party!
