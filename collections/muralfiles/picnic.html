The Picnic


The picnic was an exciting event where I was able to set up a table with the proposal on display and meet the rest of the community.


The community was excited with the idea of the murals. They asked lots of questions and became more and more excited about the idea of being able to participate in the creation of the murals.


Now that the word was spread to the parish about the Community Day on October 10th, it was time to start creating the boards!
